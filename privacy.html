2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact information: When you fill out a contact form or send us an email, we collect your name, email address, and any other details you voluntarily provide.
  • WhatsApp messaging data: When we deploy AI chatbot solutions on behalf of our business clients, end-user messages exchanged through WhatsApp may be processed to provide automated responses.   • Usage data: We may collect standard web analytics data (page views, browser type, referring URL) to understand how visitors use our website. This data is aggregated and not personally identifiable.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide our services
  • Deliver AI automation, chatbot, and workflow solutions you have contracted with us
  • Process and route WhatsApp messages through automated systems on behalf of our business clients
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Send service-related communications (not marketing without your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. WhatsApp and Messaging Services

AuraFlow builds and operates WhatsApp Business chatbot solutions using the Meta WhatsApp Cloud API. When we provide these services to a client:

  • Messages sent by end-users are processed to generate automated responses using artificial intelligence
  • Message content may be stored in our systems to maintain conversation context and improve response quality
  • We comply with Meta's WhatsApp Business Policy and applicable data protection regulations
  • End-users interacting with a chatbot we built for a third-party business should consult that business's privacy policy for details on how their data is handled

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Cloud infrastructure providers (such as hosting and database services) who process data on our behalf under confidentiality agreements
  • AI model providers: Large language model APIs (such as OpenAI) used to generate automated responses — only message content necessary for the response is transmitted
  • Meta Platforms: As required to operate WhatsApp Business API integrations
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect our rights

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Conversation data from chatbot deployments is retained for the duration of the client contract and deleted within 30 days of contract termination unless otherwise required by law or agreed upon in writing.
